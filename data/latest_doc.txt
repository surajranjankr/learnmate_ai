LearnMate AI empowers students with a private, powerful AI study tool
that works entirely offline, transforming how they interact with learning
materials.
The Challenge: Unmet Study
Needs
Students frequently encounter significant hurdles in their study routines,
impacting their efficiency and learning outcomes.
Information Overload
Struggling to extract meaningful summaries from lengthy documents
and textbooks.
Lack of Practice
Difficulty in self-assessment and knowledge reinforcement.
Contextual Q&A Limitations
Inability to get immediate, accurate answers based specifically on
their personal notes or course materials.
The Solution: LearnMate AI
LearnMate AI provides an innovative, offline-first solution to these common study challenges.
Document Input Intelligent Summaries
Accepts PDF or plain text documents. Generates concise or detailed summaries.
Interactive Quizzes Smart Chatbot
Creates quizzes with answer checking. Answers questions based solely on user notes.
Powered by the efficient Mistral-7B GGUF model and an intuitive Streamlit UI.

Chatbot Interaction: Behind the Scenes
Understanding the query process for the RAG-powered chatbot.
User Query
You ask: "What is Newton's third law?"
Semantic Search
FAISS efficiently retrieves relevant context chunks from your uploaded documents.
Prompt Generation
The retrieved context and your question are combined into a prompt for Mistral.
AI Response
Mistral generates a brief, precise answer based strictly on the provided context.
Display Answer
The response is seamlessly displayed within the Streamlit chat interface.
